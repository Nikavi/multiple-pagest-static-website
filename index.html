Buns with cinnamon filling are available in different varieties and are common in North America and Northern Europe . A variant that has spread throughout the world is the Swedish cinnamon bun, which is filled with butter or margarine , caster sugar and cinnamon and garnished with granulated sugar and possibly almond shavings.
