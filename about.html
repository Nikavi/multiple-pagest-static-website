History and variants of kanelbunle
The early history of the cinnamon roll is uncertain. In the United States , cinnamon buns are a common breakfast food . Sweet and sticky Philadelphia -style cinnamon buns originated in the 18th century and contain honey , sugar, cinnamon, and raisins . There are also many other variants, such as the Swedish which has spread globally.
In Sweden, there is evidence of wheat bread called "cinnamon bun" since at least the 1850s. The variant that was later called cinnamon bun was probably created in Sweden in the 1920s , when the availability of the ingredients increased after the First World War . However, it took until the 1950s before the cinnamon bun became common in Sweden.
The Swedish cinnamon roll is less sweet and not as powerful as American varieties. Another difference is that the Swedish cinnamon roll often contains cardamom .
